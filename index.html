About Us

Rabaul Metal Industries (RMI) is a proudly Papua New Guinea-owned company and a leading manufacturer of high-quality steel products. With strategically located branches across the New Guinea Islands — including Rabaul, Kokopo, Kimbe, Kavieng, Buka, Palmalmal, and Namatanai — we serve a wide range of industrial, residential, and commercial construction needs.

Our facilities are equipped with industry-standard machinery and operated by a skilled local workforce. We use premium-grade materials, sourced primarily from New Zealand, to ensure durability and compliance with international standards — ideal for Papua New Guinea’s demanding tropical climate.
